## Overview
[EMnify’s][1] cellular IoT connectivity platform keeps your devices connected and secure wherever you deploy. Our network-agnostic SIMs let your applications connect to more than 540 networks in over 180 countries. We also offer the largest LTE-M coverage on the market.

Use the Datadog-EMnify integration to collect metrics and data usage from IoT EMnify devices.

## Setup
Configure streaming of Usage Data following the [integration guide][2], sections “Integration Steps” and “Verifying the integration”.

Define boundaries and sensitivity, receivers for monitors.
